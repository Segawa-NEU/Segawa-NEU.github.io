My value as a programmer

My value as a programmer is that I never stop improving my skill. Whenever I find myself in difficult situation, I always strive to find a solution and optimize it, so I can do it faster and better next time I encounter the same problem. In addition, I do not believe that I can achieve the level of proficiency where I need no improvement. Because the technology is evolving at very high rate that if I stop improving, I will be left behind in the scene.

My coding philosophy

I strongly believe that better coders are those who have more tools than others. Tools are not necessity a physical tools or softwares, but are established methodology of solving problems. For instance, structural recursion is a method of traversing a self containing data structure without thinking of elaborately about the structure. Having such tool is advantageous because it saves a lot of time that could be spent on more difficult problem.
